About the System

Following operational events such as the "Iron Swords" conflict, the critical need for rapid and accurate identification of soldiers and civilians in the field became evident. Our system enables real-time face recognition for both live video feeds and pre-recorded footage. It analyzes facial features from the video and compares them to a pre-stored image database, ensuring quick and accurate identification even under varying conditions.
